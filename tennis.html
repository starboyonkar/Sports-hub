Tennis


Tennis is a racket sport played individually or in doubles.

About Tennis

  • Tennis is played using a racket and a ball.
  • It can be played as singles or doubles.
  • The objective is to hit the ball over the net within the court.
  • Tennis improves stamina, focus, and agility.
  • Popular tournaments include Wimbledon and US Open.
