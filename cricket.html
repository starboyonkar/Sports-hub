Cricket

Cricket is a popular team sport played worldwide.

About Cricket

  • Cricket is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players each.
  • It is played using a bat and a ball on a circular or oval ground.
  • The main objective is to score more runs than the opposing team.
  • Popular formats include Test Cricket, One Day Internationals (ODI), and T20.
  • Cricket is very popular in India, England, Australia, and South Africa.
